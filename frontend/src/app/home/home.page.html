A NEW WAY TO PLAY

CFB PICK6 is the newest thing in fantasy sports! The best site for your always desired college fantasy football team has finally arrived! However, there are some differences from your ordinary fantasy football site. Instead of selecting individual players, each owner selects six FBS D1 teams. The goal is to select the six teams who will win the most combined games. Don't get too arrogant yet, drafting the best squad is more difficult than you think! After drafting your squad of six, cheer them on to victory and enjoy the season. Make every game matter!
